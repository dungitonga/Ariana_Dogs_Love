Dog Avenue

Did you know? Dogs have a sense of smell that’s 40 times better than humans – they can even detect diseases!

Dogs have an extraordinary sense of smell—up to 300 million olfactory receptors (vs. about 6 million in humans)—which is why they can detect scents and even diseases that we can’t perceive!

Dogs dream just like humans, cycling through REM sleep and often twitching or vocalizing as they “act out” their dreams.
